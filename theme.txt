Looking at your AgroLinkNavbar component, here's the complete color theme breakdown:

## Primary Colors
- **Yellow/Gold Accent**: `yellow-400` and `yellow-600` - Used for the brand accent, active states, and call-to-action buttons
- **Black/Dark Background**: `black/95` - Main navbar background with 95% opacity

## Background Colors
- **Primary Background**: `bg-black/95` with `backdrop-blur-md` - Semi-transparent black with blur effect
- **Secondary Backgrounds**: 
  - `bg-gray-800/50` - 50% opacity gray for hover states and input backgrounds
  - `bg-gray-900/95` and `bg-gray-900/50` - For dropdown menus and mobile menu

## Text Colors
- **Primary Text**: `text-white` - Main brand text and input text
- **Secondary Text**: `text-gray-300` - Default nav items and secondary text
- **Accent Text**: `text-yellow-400` - Active states and hover effects
- **Muted Text**: `text-gray-400` - Placeholder text and icons
- **Error/Logout**: `text-red-400` and `text-red-300` - For logout buttons and error states
- **Button Text**: `text-black` - Text on yellow gradient buttons

## Border Colors
- **Primary Borders**: `border-gray-800/50` and `border-gray-700/50` - Subtle borders with transparency
- **Focus Borders**: `border-yellow-400/50` - Yellow accent for focused inputs

## Gradient Colors
- **Primary Gradient**: `from-yellow-400 to-yellow-600` - Used for CTA buttons and profile avatar
- **Hover Gradient**: `from-yellow-500 to-yellow-700` - Darker version for hover states
- **Active Indicator**: `from-yellow-400 to-yellow-600` - Bottom border for active nav items

## Special Effects
- **Shadows**: `shadow-yellow-400/20` - Yellow glow effects on buttons and active states
- **Notification Badge**: `bg-red-500` - Red notification indicator
- **Pulse Animation**: `bg-yellow-400` with `animate-pulse` - Animated logo accent

## Focus States
- **Ring Colors**: `ring-yellow-400/50` - Yellow focus rings for accessibility

The overall theme creates a modern, dark agricultural platform with warm yellow accents that suggest growth, harvest, and prosperity - perfect for an agricultural marketplace.
